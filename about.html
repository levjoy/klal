About K'lal

Building digital resilience for Jewish communities worldwide

Our Mission & Vision

K'lal envisions a more digitally literate and resilient Jewish community. We believe that secure and prosperous communal life is directly tied to how the people in that community understand and engage with digital tools (Internet, computers, etc.) and services (email managers, CRMs, etc).

We conduct in-depth research through surveys, guided conversations, and engagements with Jewish community actors around the world to inform our systems-level approach to delivering tailored resources to support the operational, social, and communal needs of different institutions.

Why K'lal? K'lal Yisrael (כלל ישראל) refers to unity and mutual responsibility among the Jewish people. It emphasizes the shared heritage, history, and destiny of Jews around the world. These values guide our approach to digital literacy and resilience. It is a collective effort. Only by knowing the technologies we use and our perceptions of technology as a community, can we strengthen our resilience against digital threats, ensuring technology is an enabler of communal life.

Our Core Values

Community

We believe that the strength of the Jewish community lies in our collective knowledge, action, and care for one another.

Security

We prioritize protecting the Jewish community against digital threats while maintaining the openness and connectivity that technology enables.

Education

We believe in the transformative power of knowledge and continuously work to enhance digital literacy across Jewish communities.

Innovation

We embrace technological advancements while ensuring they align with and enhance Jewish communal values and practices.
