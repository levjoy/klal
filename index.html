Digital Resilience for Jewish Communities

K'lal envisions a more digitally literate and resilient Jewish community. We believe that secure and prosperous communal life is directly tied to how the people in that community understand and engage with digital tools.

Why K'lal?

K'lal Yisrael (כלל ישראל) refers to unity and mutual responsibility among the Jewish people. These values guide our approach to digital literacy and resilience.

Community First

We believe that secure and prosperous communal life is directly tied to how the people in that community understand and engage with digital tools and services.

Our Services

We provide tailored support to strengthen the digital resilience of Jewish organizations and communities.

  • Strategic Consulting

    We provide strategic consulting on technology planning, digital security, and resilience for local and national organizations.

  • Technology Assessment

    We conduct technology assessments and deliver customized trainings to strengthen institutional resilience.

  • Curriculum Development

    We develop curricula and learning tracks for Jewish tech and security professionals and decision-makers.

  • Knowledge Repository

    We create a central repository of knowledge on technology use, best practices, and security considerations tailored to Jewish communal needs.
